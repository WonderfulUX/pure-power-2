FAQ

We know you have questions. We gathered all the information you need down below

Do I need a fabric softener?

The formulas are 'all-in-one'; however, for those who want even more softness and a longer-lasting fragrance, you can use your usual fabric softener.

Can the detergent be used in the washing machine and by hand?

Yes, it is suitable for both uses. The powder can be diluted in water for an effective hand wash.

Is the detergent suitable for delicate clothing?

The liquid detergent is 100% hypoallergenic, suitable for delicate fabrics, baby skin and underwear.

What dosage is recommended for the liquid detergent?

Use the measuring cap: Level 3: small machine / lightly soiled — Level 3 to 4: normal machine — Level 5: heavily soiled.

What dosage for the powder?

1 dose: small laundry — 2 doses: regular laundry.

What is the best product for difficult stains?

Dilute a dose of powder in a basin of water and soak the stained laundry before machine washing.
